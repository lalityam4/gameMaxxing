Kings Cup
🍺
DRINKING
4–10 players
30–60 min
Easy
Deck of cards
HOW TO PLAY
1
Place an empty cup in the center of the table. Spread a full deck of cards face-down in a circle around the cup.
2
Players take turns drawing a card from the outer ring, being careful not to break the circle of cards.
Breaking the circle = drink 3 sips as penalty
3
Each card rank has a fixed rule. Ace = Waterfall, 2 = You, 3 = Me, 4 = Floor, 5 = Guys, 6 = Chicks, 7 = Heaven, 8 = Mate, 9 = Rhyme, 10 = Categories.
4
Jack = Rule (make a new game rule). Queen = Questions (ask questions; first to answer drinks). The player who draws a King pours into the center cup and drinks it when the 4th King is drawn.
5
The unlucky player who draws the final (4th) King must drink the entire center cup — whatever mixture is inside from all previous pours.
The game ends after the 4th King is drawn
PRO VARIATION
Try "Custom Rules" — each player writes their own rule for one card value before the game starts. Reveal rules only when that card is drawn for chaotic results.
