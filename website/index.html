Your data & your rights

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

GameMaxxing is a fully offline iOS application. We have built it with privacy as a core principle: the app does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data you create within the app — including custom games, custom categories, scores, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your own device using iOS standard storage mechanisms.

This Privacy Policy explains what we do and do not do with your information, and is intended to be plain and straightforward.

Information We Do Not Collect

GameMaxxing does not collect any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect or have access to:

— Your name, email address, phone number, or any contact information
— Your location or device location data
— Usage analytics, session data, or behavioral tracking
— Crash reports or diagnostic logs sent to external servers
— Device identifiers or advertising IDs
— Any account credentials (there is no account system)

We do not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or any tracking frameworks.

Local Storage

Any data you enter into GameMaxxing — such as custom games, custom categories, hidden game preferences, and in-app settings — is stored locally on your iPhone using iOS UserDefaults and standard local persistence mechanisms. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed from your device in accordance with standard iOS behavior. We have no copy of that data and cannot recover it.

Third-Party Services

GameMaxxing does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, APIs, or cloud platforms. There are no embedded analytics libraries, advertising networks, social media SDKs, or crash reporting tools in the app. Your use of the app does not create any data relationship with any company other than Apple, which governs the iOS operating system on your device through their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

GameMaxxing includes content related to drinking games and is intended for users who are 17 years of age or older, in accordance with the App Store age rating. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Because the app collects no personal data whatsoever, there is no special risk to children's information; however, parental discretion is advised regarding the nature of certain game content in the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app following the posting of changes constitutes your acceptance of those changes.

Because we do not collect your contact information, we cannot notify you of updates by email — all changes are published here.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: support@gamemaxxing.app

We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries within a reasonable timeframe.
